About Me

My name is Michael James Waldron. I have a daughter in this picture that is cooler than I will ever be. I am a Carpenter and curiouser vessel.

Craft is something I feel connected to. I can't quite explain it but all through my life I have been attracted to the quality of shaping something with my hands. I have built and produced plays and other theatre expereinces for free and for high dollar money. Operas, Art projects and Plays have all had an impact on my professional scope.
